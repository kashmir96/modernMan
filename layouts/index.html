Frequently Asked Questions

Do you allow walk-ins?

Yes, our barbershop allows walk-ins. Pop by at the Crossing in Christchurch CBD, and we'll squeeze you in between our other bookings.

What products does the Modern Man barbershop use?

We use a range of high-quality grooming products tailored for men. Our barbers can recommend the best products for your specific needs.

What services are available at the barbershop?

Our barbershop offers a variety of services, including haircuts, beard trims, hot towel shaves, and styling consultations. Visit us for a complete list of services.

What payment methods are available?

We accept cash, credit cards, and mobile payment apps like Apple Pay and Google Pay. Choose the payment method that's most convenient for you.

Where are you located?

We are located at The Crossing in Christchurch CBD. Our address is 123 Barber Street, Christchurch, New Zealand.

Where should I park?

There are parking spaces available near our barbershop. You can find parking in the surrounding streets or use nearby parking lots.
